Implementing SMR: Design Considerations and Best Practices

Source: Western Digital

Optimizing Shingled Magnetic Recording

Unlocking Higher Storage Density: Essential guide for integrating SMR drives in data centers and archives.

  • SMR Basics: Overlaps tracks like shingles for up to 25% more capacity in HDDs.
  • Design Considerations: Zone-based writing; metadata management for band updates; host-managed vs. drive-managed SMR.
  • Best Practices: Use sequential writes; implement shingled caching; monitor write amplification.
  • Benefits: Cost-effective scaling for cold storage, backups, and big data; supports ZAC/ZBC standards.
  • Integration Tips: Pair with SSD caching; firmware updates for compatibility; testing for workload patterns.

SMR enables efficient, high-capacity storage solutions, ideal for cloud and enterprise environments seeking sustainable data growth.
